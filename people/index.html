Corey C. Holt

Lecturer/Assistant Professor

Corey, originally from Wales, completed his undergraduate degree and PhD at the University of Exeter. He was mostly based at the Centre for Environment, Fisheries, and Aquaculture Science (CEFAS), where he characterised microbes impacting lobster production using a novel sea-based culture system. He later moved to Canada to explore more fundamental questions of host-microbe associations in invertebrates, with a particular focus of protist diversity.

If you’re interested in joining the Holt Lab, please send Corey an email. Before you do, please take a look at our Lab Values.
